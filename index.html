📖 Quick guide — how to write notes
  • Letters are notes: C D E F G A B (uppercase = lower octave, lowercase c d e = higher).
  • Longer note: add a number — C2 is twice as long, C4 a whole note.
  • Bar line: use | to split into measures (just for tidiness).
  • Rest (silence): the letter z.
  • Sharp / flat: ^C is C-sharp, _B is B-flat.
  • Higher / lower octave: c' goes up, C, goes down.
